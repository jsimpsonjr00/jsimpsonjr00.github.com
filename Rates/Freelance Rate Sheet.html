Notes
Hours billed in 6 minute increments. Bundled hours must occur within specified time frames. No refunds for unused time. Excess hours shall be billed at the Hourly rate after notification agreed bundled time is exhausted.




Gigs and New Client Invoicing
New clients and one off gigs require a start work payment of at minimum 1 hrs labor and up to 100% of fixed price or expected hourly billing. Fixed price gigs are billed in milestones dependent upon the size of the project and negotiated on a per gig basis.




Regular Client Invoicing
Regular clients are offered invoicing on a bi-weekly basis via e-mail. All invoices are due in full upon receipt.




Payment Methods Accepted
Payment shall be provided by Paypal or Dwolla. Long term clients are offered the option of check or Direct Deposit payments.
